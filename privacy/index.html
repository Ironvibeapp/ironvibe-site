Privacy Policy

Last updated for IronVibe app release. Full version available at ironvibe.app/privacy.

Introduction

IronVibe is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information. All workout data is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

Your workout history, exercise logs, and any settings are kept only on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers.

Third‑party services

We may use Google Analytics or Firebase for crash reporting to improve app stability. Such services may collect anonymous technical data (e.g. device type, app version) and are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or IronVibe, contact us at support@ironvibe.app.
